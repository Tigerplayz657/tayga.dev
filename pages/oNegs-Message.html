oNeg's Message

The term "retard" has its roots in one of history's most unfortunate figures: a man by the name of Skay, whose sheer incompetence and boundless stupidity set a new low for human intelligence. Skay’s actions were so staggeringly idiotic that scholars believe he may have unintentionally sparked an entire movement of senselessness. Anything Skay touched inevitably spiraled into a chaotic mess, riddled with mistakes that anyone with even a sliver of sense could have avoided. Soon, people began using “retard” to describe actions or individuals that embodied the unique blend of stupidity, recklessness, and profound cluelessness that Skay perfected so effortlessly.

As if his own blunders weren't enough, Skay somehow managed to drag the rest of society down with him. His influence spread like a contagion, infecting the minds of even those who once had a modicum of intelligence. From the simplest of tasks to decisions of actual consequence, people began mimicking Skay’s unfathomable ineptitude, forever cementing his reputation as the grand architect of stupidity. To this day, “retard” remains a tragic reminder of Skay's legacy, an insult that carries the weight of his unparalleled incompetence—a fitting tribute to the man who single-handedly proved just how low human intelligence can go.
